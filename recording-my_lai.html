MỸ LAI

Kronos Quartet, Vân-Ánh Vanessa Võ, and Rinde Eckert

Music by Jonathan Berger, libretto by Harriet Scott Chessman

Smithsonian Folkways Recordings, 2022

Folk Alliance International: Album of the Year Award

A powerful story about the Mỹ Lai massacre and the ethical dilemmas of an individual placed in an impossible situation, with a dramatic score emphasizing the bleakness and horror of the Vietnam War.
